{\bf 7.6}\\
	$\displaystyle Prob\Big(h(x)=z\Big)=\sum_{i=0}^M{Prob\Big(h(x)=z,h(y)=i\Big)}$\\
	$\because \forall ~Prob\Big(h(x)=z,h(y)=w\Big)=\dfrac{1}{(M+1)^2}$\\
	$\therefore Prob\Big(h(x)=z\Big)=\dfrac{1}{M+1}$
\qed\\

{\bf 7.10}\\
	令$p_i$表示扔了$i$次硬币才扔出正面的概率\\
	$p_i=\left(1-p\right)^{i-1}p$\\
	$\displaystyle E=\sum_{i=1}^{\infty}ip_i=\sum_{i=1}^{\infty}i\left(1-p\right)^{i-1}p=\frac{1}{p}$
\qed\\
