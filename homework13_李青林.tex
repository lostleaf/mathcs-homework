{\bf 6.1}\\
	令$w=(1,1,\cdots,1,-\frac{1}{2})$\\
	令$k_i$表示$a_i$中非零元素的个数,显然$1\leq{}k_i\leq{d+1}$\\
	$\delta=min\left\{\left|\dfrac{w\cdot{}a_i}{|w|}\right|\right\}=\dfrac{\sqrt{k_i}}{\sqrt{d+1/4}}$\\
	$\Longrightarrow\delta=\Omega(\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{d}})=\Omega(\dfrac{1}{d})$
\qed\\

{\bf 6.4}\\
	不妨设有偶数分量为1时标记为$-1$,奇数个时为$+1$
	反之存在$w,b$\\
	使
  	$
  		\quad
  		\left\{
	   		\begin{aligned}
   				\overset{}
   				w\cdot{}(0,0,0,0,\cdots,0)<b\\
				w\cdot{}(1,1,0,0,\cdots,0)<b\\
				w\cdot{}(0,1,0,0,\cdots,0)>b\\
				w\cdot{}(1,0,0,0,\cdots,0)>b
   			\end{aligned}
   		\right.
   	$
   	成立\\
   	$\Longrightarrow 0>b>0 $，矛盾
\qed\\

	如果此时存在第二个点也在圆周上，$C_2$已找到\\
