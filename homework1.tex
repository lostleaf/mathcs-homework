{\bf 2.11}\\
对$\forall{d}\in{\mathbf{N}}$\\
若$d=2k(k\in{\mathbf{N}})~~~~~~~ \Gamma(d)=(k-1)!$\\
若$d=2k+1(k\in{\mathbf{N}})~~ \Gamma(d)=(k-1)!\sqrt{\pi}$\\
则对于$\forall{d}=2k,V(d)=\dfrac{\pi^{k}}{k*(k-1)!}=\dfrac{\pi^k}{k!} $\\
对于$\forall{d}=2k+1,V(d)=\dfrac{\pi^{k+1/2}}{\pi^{1/2}*(k+1/2)*\prod_{i=1}^{k}(i-\frac{1}{2})}=
\dfrac{\pi^k}{\prod_{i=1}^{k+1}(i-\frac{1}{2})}$\\
~\newline$\therefore$
$\dfrac{V(2k+2)}{V(2k)}=\dfrac{\pi}{k+1}$~~~~~~~~
$\dfrac{V(2k+3)}{V(2k+1)}=\dfrac{\pi}{k+3/2}$\\
$\therefore k=3$时,$V(2k)$最大~~~$k=2$时$V(2k+1)$最大\\
$\therefore V_{max}=$Max$\left( V(5),V(6) \right)=\dfrac{8\pi^2}{15}$
\hfill$\Box$\\

{\bf 2.12}\\
$V(r,d)=r^d\dfrac{\pi^{d/2}}{\frac{d}{2}\Gamma(\frac{d}{2})}$\\
当$r=2$时,体积先增加，再减小，最后收敛于0\\
当$r\neq2$但为常数时,同上\\
若$V(r,d)\equiv{}v,r=\left({\dfrac{vd~\Gamma(\frac{d}{2})}{2\pi^{d/2}}}\right)^\frac{1}{d}$
\hfill$\Box$\\

{\bf 2.17}\\
令$f(x)$表示圆柱体积,设高度为h\\
底面积为$(1-h^2)^{\frac{d-1}{2}}V(d-1)$\\
$f(x)=h(1-h^2)^{\frac{d-1}{2}}V(d-1)$\\
当$f(x)'=(1-h^2)^{\frac{d-3}{2}}(1-dh^2)V(d-1)=0$时,$f(x)$取极大值\\
得$h=\frac{1}{\sqrt{d}}$或$h=1$\\
$\because{}h=1,f(1)=0$,不可能为极大值\\
$\therefore{}h=\frac{1}{\sqrt{d}}$时,f(x)取极大值
\hfill$\Box$\\
